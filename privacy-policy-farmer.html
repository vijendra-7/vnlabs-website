Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 8, 2026

📋 Introduction

Welcome to Farmer Accounting - ખેડૂત હિસાબ. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our application.

📊 Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Local Data: All accounting data, transactions, crop entries, farmer details, and pdf reports are created and stored locally on your device using a local SQLite database.

Automatically Collected Information

  • Usage Data & Crash Reports: App interactions, crash reports, and performance logs if you opt-in to system analytics provided by Google.

🔒 How We Use Your Information

Our app focuses on data privacy by storing accounting records ONLY on your device. We use this data strictly for:

  • Allowing you to manage your ledgers and accounting within the app.
  • Generating PDF reports and invoices summarizing your activity locally.

☁️ Local Storage and PDF Generation

Important: Farmer Accounting does NOT transmit, store, or back up your financial data to any external cloud servers by itself.

When you use the app:

  • We request local storage permissions to be able to save generated accounting PDF reports directly onto your phone's memory.
  • You are solely responsible for keeping a backup of the PDF exports or device data in case of device replacement or uninstallation.

🛡️ Data Security

We respect your privacy by keeping your data localized on your device. To ensure safety:

  • All data is stored inside the app’s secure private sandbox directory by the OS.
  • We do not collect or sell any personal or financial data.

Since your phone acts as the sole storage mechanism for the database, please make sure your device is secure (e.g., using a screen lock).

👥 Data Sharing

We do not collect, sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We do not have servers to access your data.

👶 Children's Privacy

Farmer Accounting is intended for agricultural professionals and farmers. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

🔔 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on our website and updating the "Last updated" date.
