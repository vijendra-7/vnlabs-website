Privacy Policy

Plant Sanctuary

Last updated: March 20, 2026

Introduction

Welcome to Plant Sanctuary by vnlabs. We are dedicated to providing a premium experience for plant lovers while respecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines our data handling practices for the Plant Sanctuary mobile application.

Data Sovereignty

Your plant data belongs to you. Our app is designed with a "local-first" philosophy.

  • Offline Storage: All plant details, watering records, and sanctuary notes are stored securely on your device's internal storage using Hive.
  • No Cloud Sync: We do NOT transmit your personal plant data or care schedules to our servers. Your data stays on your phone.

Notifications

The app uses local notifications to remind you to water your plants. These reminders are scheduled and triggered entirely on your device and do not involve any remote push servers.

Permissions

We may request access to your Camera and Photo Library to allow you to add photos of your plants. These photos are stored locally and are never shared with VN Labs.

Children's Privacy

Plant Sanctuary is safe for all ages. We do not collect any personal information from any users, including children under 13.

Contact Us

For questions or feedback regarding your privacy, please contact us at vnlabs.in@gmail.com.
