Why we started

During GATE prep, we couldn't find a simple way to track our progress. Everything was either too complicated or tried to do too much.

So we built GATE Tracker. Just chapter tracking, reminders, and cloud sync. Nothing fancy, but it worked.

That's what we do now. Find problems students face, build simple solutions, share them for free.

How we work

We're students building for students. We experiment, test, and iterate based on what actually helps.

Each product starts as an experiment. If it's useful, we polish it and release it. If not, we learn and move on.

Keep it simple

Good tools don't need complex features. They just need to work well at what they're meant to do.

Stay curious

We're always learning. Every project is a chance to experiment and try new things.

Make it free

We believe good tools should be accessible. Everything we build is free, no strings attached.

What's next

We're working on more tools for students. Some will work out, some won't. That's the nature of experiments.
