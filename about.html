Who We Are

Building tools we
wish existed

No big vision statements or grand plans — just building stuff that solves real problems for students.

Why we started

During our GATE prep, we couldn't find a simple way to track our progress. Everything was either too complicated or tried to do too much.

So we built GATE Tracker. Just chapter tracking, reminders, and cloud sync. Nothing fancy, but it worked perfectly for our specific use case.

That essentially became our model. Find problems students face, build simple solutions, and share them for free.

How we work

We're students building for students. We experiment, test, and iterate based on what actually helps.

Each product starts as an experiment. If it's useful, we polish it and release it. If not, we learn from it and move on.

Keep it simple

Good tools don't need complex features. They just need to work well at what they're meant to do.

Stay curious

We're always learning. Every project is a chance to experiment and try completely new things.

Make it free

We believe good tools should be accessible. Everything we build is free, no strings attached.

What's next?

We're working on more tools for students. Some will work out perfectly, some won't. That's the nature of experiments. Want to follow along?
