FreshTrack is built from the ground up to respect your data. No cloud, no tracking, just a local app that helps you manage your pantry.

100% Local Storage

Every single item you add to your inventory, every category you create, and every expiration date you track lives only on your physical device. FreshTrack has zero cloud databases. We cannot see your data because it never leaves your phone.

  • Data stored directly in OS sandbox memory
  • No transmission to external servers
  • You maintain total control and ownership

Zero Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, rent, or distribute your personal information to third parties. Our business model is building tools that work, not harvesting user intelligence.

Analytics & Metrics

We rely strictly on the standard, anonymized app metrics provided by Google Play/Apple App Store. No invasive third-party analytics SDKs are embedded into FreshTrack.

No Accounts

No emails. No passwords. Just open the app and start tracking instantly.

Child Privacy

Safe for all ages. We knowingly collect exactly zero data from children under 13.
