Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 8, 2026

📋 Introduction

Welcome to Master Attendance. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data when you use our application.

📊 Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Local Data: All attendance records, subjects, schedules, and analytics are created and stored locally on your device.

Automatically Collected Information

  • Usage Data: Basic app interactions and performance metrics (if you opt-in to system analytics provided by Google/Apple).

🔒 How We Use Your Information

Our app focuses on data privacy by storing your attendance records ONLY on your device. We use this data strictly for:

  • Allowing you to track your lecture attendance and manage your classes within the app.
  • Generating attendance analytics and reports summarizing your activity locally.

☁️ Local Storage

Important: Master Attendance does NOT transmit, store, or back up your attendance data to any external cloud servers.

When you use the app:

  • All data is stored directly on your phone's memory.
  • You are solely responsible for keeping a backup of the data if you change devices. Uninstalling the app may result in permanent data loss.

🛡️ Data Security

We respect your privacy by keeping your data localized on your device. To ensure safety:

  • All data is stored inside the app’s secure private sandbox directory by the OS.
  • We do not collect or sell any personal data.

👥 Data Sharing

We do not collect, sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We do not have servers to access your data.

👶 Children's Privacy

Master Attendance is intended for students tracking their classes. While people of all ages can use the tool, we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 since no data is sent to our servers.

🔔 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on our website and updating the "Last updated" date.
