Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 24, 2026

This Privacy Policy governs the use of the mobile application 12 Board (hereinafter referred to as the "App"). The App is a minimal, offline utility designed to help students organize, track, and monitor their Class 12 Board Exam preparation.

1. Personal Information Collection & Sharing

We believe in absolute data privacy. The App does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personally identifiable information (PII) or user data.

There are:

  • No account registrations or user logins.
  • No tracking mechanisms or analytics SDKs.
  • No connections to remote database servers.

2. Local Storage on Device

All data you input – including your syllabus checklist completion, Pomodoro study sessions, customized formula notes, and daily targets – is stored locally and exclusively on your own mobile device's internal storage via standard persistent preferences. No data ever leaves your device.

3. Data Deletion & Retention

Since your study progress data is stored locally on your device, it is retained until you choose to delete it. You can instantly erase 100% of the stored data by clearing the App's data from your device's settings or by uninstalling the App.

4. Internet Permissions

The App does not require or request network permissions, and runs completely offline.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. Your continued use of the App constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns regarding this Privacy Policy or the App, please feel free to reach out to us via email or Play Store support contact.
