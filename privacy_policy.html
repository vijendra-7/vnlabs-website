Class 12th Board Tracker

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 24, 2026

This Privacy Policy outlines how the Class 12th Board Tracker mobile application (referred to as the "App") protects and respects your privacy. Built exclusively as a local utility helper, Class 12th Board Tracker is dedicated to helping students track and manage their Class 12 Board Exam preparation offline.

1. Zero Data Collection & Transmission

We hold privacy as a fundamental value. The App is structured so that it never collects, processes, transmits, or shares any of your personal data, identifier information, or usage habits.

  • No accounts, registrations, or logins are required.
  • No analytics trackers, crash-reporting SDKs, or third-party cookies.
  • No server connections or database synchronizations.

2. Pure Local Device Storage

Any information you input – including your checklist completions, revision logs, timer sessions, custom formula sheets, and study goals – is saved exclusively and securely in your device's local memory via persistent storage preferences. This data is private to you and never leaves your device.

3. Data Deletion and Control

You have full, absolute control over your data. Because all information resides on your device, you can completely erase it at any time by:

  • Clearing the App's storage cache from your Android settings.
  • Uninstalling the App, which automatically wipes all local data permanently.

4. Zero Network Requirements

The App is designed to run entirely in an offline environment and does not request or require internet permissions.

5. Policy Updates

We may periodically update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in compliance requirements. Any modifications will be indicated by updating the date at the top of this document.

6. Contact & Support

If you have any questions, feedback, or concerns regarding this policy, feel free to reach out to us via our developer contact page or support options on the Play Store.
