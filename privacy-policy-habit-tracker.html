Privacy Policy

Habit Tracker

Last updated: March 8, 2026

Introduction

Welcome to Habit Tracker by vnlabs. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding data collection when you use our application.

Information We Collect

Our fundamental principle is that your habit data is yours completely.

  • Data Stored Locally: All your habits, streaks, completions, settings and configuration are stored entirely locally on your device using shared preferences.
  • No Data Collection: We do NOT require an account to use Habit Tracker. We do NOT collect PII. We do NOT transmit your habit data or usage logs to any external servers.

How We Protect Your Info

Because your data never leaves your device and is not saved on any cloud servers managed by vnlabs, it is completely secure. Only individuals with physical access to your device can view your habits.

Data Sharing

Since we do not collect any personal data, we do not sell, trade, rent, or share any of your information to third parties.

Your Rights

You have full control over your data. You can completely clear your data at any time by uninstalling the app or clearing the app's local data from your settings.

Children's Privacy

Habit Tracker is general audience software. We do not intentionally gather any private data, and particularly do not intentionally gather information from children under 13.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at vnlabs.in@gmail.com.
