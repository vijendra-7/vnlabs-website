Privacy Policy

GATE Tracker

Last updated: December 13, 2025

Introduction

Welcome to GATE Tracker. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our application.

Information We Collect

Account Information: When you sign in with Google, we collect your name, email address, and profile picture.

Study Data: Progress tracking, subject selections, chapter completions, to-do lists, and study schedules. Your selected exam branch and target exam date.

Usage Data: App interactions, feature usage, device type, operating system version, and app version.

How We Use Your Info

  • To provide and maintain our service
  • To sync your study progress across devices
  • To send you notifications about your study goals and exam countdown
  • To improve and personalize your experience
  • To analyze app usage and performance
  • To provide customer support

Google Drive Integration

Important: GATE Tracker uses Google Drive to backup and restore your study data. We only access files created by our app in your Google Drive.

  • We request the drive.file scope, which allows us to access only files created by GATE Tracker.
  • We cannot access any other files in your Google Drive.
  • Backup files are stored in a dedicated app folder in your Google Drive.
  • You can delete backup files at any time from your Google Drive.
  • We do not share your Google Drive data with any third parties.

Data Security

We implement industry-standard encryption, secure authentication via Google Sign-In, and strict internal access limitations. However, note that no transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

Your Rights & Retention

We retain your info as long as your account is active. You have the right to access, export, correct, or delete your data anytime, or revoke Google Drive permissions.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. Data is only shared to comply with laws or via secure essential service providers.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at vnlabs.in@gmail.com.
