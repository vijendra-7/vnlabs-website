üîß How to Delete Your Data

Follow these simple steps to permanently delete all your data from GATE Tracker:

1

Open the App

Launch the GATE Tracker app on your device and make sure you're signed in to your account.

2

Navigate to Settings

Tap on the Settings icon in the app's navigation menu (usually located at the bottom or in the hamburger menu).

3

Scroll to Account Section

In the Settings screen, scroll down to find the "Account" or "Data Management" section.

4

Tap "Delete All Data"

Look for the "Delete All Data" button. It will be clearly marked with a red color to indicate it's a destructive action.

5

Confirm Deletion

The app will show you a confirmation dialog explaining what will be deleted. Read it carefully, then confirm that you want to proceed with the deletion.

6

Wait for Completion

The app will delete all your data from our servers. This process usually takes just a few seconds. You'll see a confirmation message when the deletion is complete.

‚úÖ Deletion Complete

Once the process is complete, you'll be signed out of the app. All your data has been permanently removed from our servers.

üîÑ Alternative Options

Before deleting all your data, consider these alternatives:

Sign Out Instead

If you just want to stop using the app temporarily, you can simply sign out. Your data will remain stored, and you can sign back in anytime to continue where you left off.

Switch Branch

If you want to start fresh with a different GATE branch (CS/CE/DA), you can use the "Switch Branch" option in Settings instead of deleting everything.

Export Your Data First

Consider creating a backup of your data before deletion. You can use the Google Drive backup feature to save a copy of your progress.

‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

Can I recover my data after deletion?

No. Data deletion is permanent and irreversible. Once deleted, we cannot recover your information.

How long does the deletion process take?

The deletion is immediate and usually completes within a few seconds. However, it may take up to 24 hours for all data to be completely removed from our backup systems.

Will deleting data remove the app from my device?

No, this only deletes your data from our servers. To remove the app from your device, you need to uninstall it separately.

What happens to my Google account connection?

Your Google account connection will be revoked. If you want to completely remove GATE Tracker's access to your Google account, you should also visit your Google Account Permissions page and revoke access there.

I deleted my data by mistake. What can I do?

Unfortunately, there's nothing we can do to recover deleted data. If you had a Google Drive backup, you might be able to restore from that, but server-side data cannot be recovered.
